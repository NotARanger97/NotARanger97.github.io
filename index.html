Dokkan APT calculator

Insert the data for the unit you want to calculate the apt, then press the button Calculate



How to use:

  • ATK Value: here you put the unit ATK value in which you are interested in.
  • Leader Skill: put the Leader skill multiplier.
  • Link Gain: it's the total of links that you want to have active during the calculation, no links active is 0
  • Passive Gain: It consist of passive Gain before and After Super Attack, if a unit Has only a passive Gain at start of turn you can leave The Passive Gain on SA field empty and viceversa, if it's both you have to insert both values
  • Ki Multiplier: Here you put the Ki Multiplier a unit has, if it's 150% the number to put here is 150 for example. Please refer to the Dokkan Wiki for more info on what ki multiplier means.
  • Additional Chanche and Crit Chanche: here you put the total additional and crit chanche from the potential system and passive, for example if a unit has no crit chanche from passive you can leave it empty and viceversa. Medium is 25, high 50 and great is 70
  • Disclaimer This is not a 100% Accurate APT calc, you can use this tool to have an idea of what a unit can accomplish, but actual numbers may differ, this tool cannot currently calc correctly units with additional super attacks in passive or units with counters.
