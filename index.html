Dokkan calculator

Insert the data for the unit you want to calculate the apt, then press the button

How to use:

  • ATK Value: here you put the unit ATK value in which you are interested in.
  • Leader Skill: put the Leader skill multiplier, if you have 2 170% leaders the multiplier is 4.4 (1.7*2 + 1).
  • Link Gain: it's the total of links that you want to have active during the calculation, no links active is 1, only fierce battle (20% at lvl 10) is 1.2 and so on.
  • Passive Gain: it's the total of the passive gain a unit gets, if a unit gets a boost pre and post SA, you have to multiply them together (2.2 pre SA and 1.8 post SA means you have to put here 3.96). Ex: a passive of atk +80% is 1.8 (0.8 + 1) 120% is 2.2 and so on.
  • Ki Multiplier: Here you put the Ki Multiplier a unit has, if it's 150% the number to put here is 1.5, for example. Please refer to the dokkan wiki for more info.
  • Additional Chanche and Crit Chanche: here you put the total additional and crit chanche from the potential system or passive(50% means 0.5, 20% 0.2, 100% 1).
  • Damage Multiplier: here you put the total damage multiplier, so th sum of hidden potential, Unit Damage(immense, supreme) and SA effects (Raises Attack).
